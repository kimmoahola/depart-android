Depart shows you where the local bus, tram or train is right now in real-time. Also you can view locations of the stops and open their timetables. Currently are shown only few locations:

  • Buses, trams and their stops from Helsinki region (HSL)
  • Trains and train stations from Finland (VR)
  • Buses and their stops from Tampere
  • Bus stops from Turku
